Our Slogans

The spirit of our struggle and the vision we share for a just and prosperous Nepal.

Home / Our Slogans

At Rastriya Shramik Mahasangh Nepal, our slogans are the heartbeat of our movement. They carry the voices of workers, ignite our resolve, and unite us in the fight for justice and dignity across Nepal.

Slogans are not just words we chant; they are the spirit of our struggle, the call for solidarity, and the promise of a better tomorrow for every worker.

— Rastriya Shramik Mahasangh Nepal

1

नेपाल आमाको जय!

Nepal Aama ko Jay!

2

देशको हितमा गर्छौँ काम, कामको लिन्छौँ पूरा दाम!

Deshko Heetma Garchau Kaam, Kaam ko Linchau Pura Daam!

3

श्रमको पुंजी, श्रमको मान, मागिरहेछ मजदुर किसान!

Shramko Punji, Shramko Maan, Mangirahecha Majdoor Kishaan!

4

को बनाउछ नेपाल महान? मजदुर किसान! मजदुर किसान!

Ko Banaucha Nepal Mahan? Majdoor Kishaan! Majdoor Kishaan!

5

श्रमिक संसारलाई एक गरौँ!

Shramik Sansar lai Ek Garau!

6

सरकारवाद, पुंजीवाद... शोषण गर्छन् एक समान!

Sarkarbas, Punjibad... Shoshan Garchan Ek Saman!

7

राष्ट्रभक्त श्रमिक... एक हौँ, एक हौँ!

Rastrabhakt Shramik... Ek hau, Ek Hau!

8

ठेकाप्रथा समाप्त गर! समाप्त गर! पैसा र पसिनाको मूल्य सम्झाऔँ एक समान

Thekapratha Samapta Gara! Samapta Gara! Paisa ra Pasinako Mulya Samjhau Ek Samaan

Rastriya Shramik Mahasangh
